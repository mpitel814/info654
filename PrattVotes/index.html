* We are proud to provide Pratt students with clear and accurate information regarding all aspects of the voting process *

We encourage you to get involved in local govermnent and your community. Our site aims to bring together each piece of data that a voter would need to make confident and educated decisions. This consists of registration information, a poll locator, answers to frequently asked voting inquiries, details about current and upcoming administration, as well as a quiz to test & inform your voting knowlegde. Your vote matters and we are here to help. This educational resource was crafted by five members of the the Pratt student body.
